Frequently Asked Questions

Find Answers to Common Questions

What areas do you deliver to?

We deliver to all major construction sites within a 50-mile radius of our store. For locations outside this range, please contact us for special arrangements.

What equipment is available for rental?

We offer a wide range of construction equipment for rental, including power tools, scaffolding, generators, and specialized construction machinery. Contact us for a complete list of available equipment.

How do I request a consultation?

You can request a consultation by filling out our contact form, calling us directly, or visiting our store. Our team will get back to you within 24 hours to schedule a consultation.

Do you offer bulk discounts?

Yes, we offer special pricing for bulk orders. The discount varies based on the quantity and type of materials. Contact our sales team for a custom quote.
